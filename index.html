Edinburgh Perl Mongers

Mostly an excuse to meet up once a month for a $beverage and chat amongst like minded individuals. The conversation covers a wide range of subjects, technical and otherwise, so anyone is welcome who knows their $foo from their #include.

Established in July 1999, we're a part of the global Perl Monger organisation.

Events

  • Regular social meeting: 4th Thursday of the month. From 6:30pm at Cumberland Bar.
  • Irregular social meeting: 5th Thursday of the month. Check the mailing list as venues change.
  • YAPC::Europe 2013 in Kiev — “Future Perl”
