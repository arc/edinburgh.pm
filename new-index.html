What is Edinburgh Perl Mongers?
Edinburgh Perl Mongers was established in July 1999 to provide Perl coders in the Central Scotland area with a forum for educating, problem solving, job seeking, collaboration, socialising and whatever else you fancy.

Edinburgh Perl Mongers is part of the global set of Perl Monger groups.

Book reviews
From time to time we get review copies of new publications. The write ups for those books can be found in the book reviews section. If you would like to submit a review for inclusion in this section, or perhaps obtain a review copy of a new book, then please contact Rory Macdonald.
